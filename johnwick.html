About John Wick

John Wick is a legendary hitman who retired from his violent career after marrying the love of his life. His peaceful life is shattered when a gangster steals his car and kills his dog, the last gift from his wife. John unleashes his fury on the criminal underworld and becomes the most feared assassin.
